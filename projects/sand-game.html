📖 How It Works

Draw on the canvas to create falling sand, flowing water, and other elements. Watch them interact with realistic physics!

🧪 Materials & Physics

  • Sand (Gold): Falls down, slides diagonally, sinks through water/acid
  • Water (Blue): Falls and spreads horizontally, flows around obstacles
  • Wall (Gray): Solid barrier that blocks all movement
  • Acid (Green): Flows like water but dissolves sand and walls on contact
  • Eraser: Remove any material from the canvas

🎮 Controls

  • Click and drag on the canvas to paint materials
  • Use the material buttons to switch between elements
  • Adjust brush size with the slider
  • Clear canvas to start fresh
